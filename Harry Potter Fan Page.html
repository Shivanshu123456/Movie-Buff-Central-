HARRY POTTER FAN PAGE

        Harry Potter is a fictional character and the protagonist of J.K. Rowling's globally acclaimed book series.
        The story begins with Harry, an orphaned boy who lives with his unpleasant relatives, the Dursleys.
        On his eleventh birthday, he learns that he is a wizard and receives an invitation to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
        Throughout the series, Harry navigates the challenges of magical education, makes loyal friends like Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, and confronts 
the dark wizard 
Voldemort, who is responsible for his parents' deaths.
        The series blends fantasy, adventure, and themes of bravery, friendship, and the struggle between good and evil.
